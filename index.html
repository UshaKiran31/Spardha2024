    SPARDHA

    SPARDHA 2K24

    VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted an annual Tech Fest, SPARDHA on August 22nd and 23rd. The tech events span two days and comprise two spot events and four major events. The goal of Spardha 2K24 is to offer a platform for students' overall growth. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and ten additional professional societies are encouraged by VVIT for its various student programmes. VVIT has two ACM chapters, VVIT-ACM-Women and VVIT-ACM-Guntur, within the Departments of CSE, IT, CIC, AID, CSM & CSO. Participants include the student body from Andhra Pradesh's 36 engineering colleges. This festival is attended by students from our college as well as students from other colleges.

    About ACM

    The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is an international learned society for computing. It is the world's largest and one of the most significant computing societies. There are various membership categories, including Professional, Student, and Special Memberships. ACM will conduct many annual events , Friday events will be conducted during the clubs and certificates will also be provided. From ACM 3 annual events will be conducted , Spardha , Avega, Hour of Code. Avega will be conducted on the occasion of engineers day ( sep 15 ).Hour of Code is something different, we go to the near by schools to educate them with various topics. ACM Members receive access to a vast digital library, discounted conference fees, networking opportunities, career resources, and more. Digital Library: A comprehensive online resource providing access to ACM’s publications, conference proceedings, technical magazines, newsletters, and multimedia files. Journals and Magazines: ACM publishes a wide range of journals and magazines such as "Communications of the ACM," "ACM Transactions," and "ACM Computing Surveys." ACM organizes numerous conferences globally. Offers resources for continuing education, including courses, webinars, and certifications. ACM offers various volunteer opportunities for members to get involved, from organizing events and conferences. ACM is not only for Indians , but it has members and chapters in numerous countries, facilitating a global exchange of knowledge and collaboration in computing.
