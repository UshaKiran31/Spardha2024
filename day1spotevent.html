CRYPTIC CLUE

-"Adventure Awaits"

22 August, Thursday

Event Type:

Spot Event

Team Size:

About This Event


This is a fun and engaging word-guessing game where participants must deduce the word based on a series of images. These images could be blurry, or even a combination of both. The participant who guesses the most words correctly wins the game.

BALL FLEE

-"Spot The Misfit"

22 August, Thursday

Event Type:

Spot Event

Team Size:

1

About This Event


An exciting game where participants are given a board filled with balls. The goal is to remove all the balls from the board without allowing the odd-colored ball to fall through the hole in the middle. The participant who clears the board first is declared the winner.

BREEZE BLAST

-"Blow, Strike,
Win The Fight!"

22 August, Thursday

Event Type:

Spot Event

Team Size:

1

About This Event


Participants compete simultaneously in this thrilling game. Each player inflates a balloon and then uses the released air to knock cups off a table. The first player to clear all the cups from the table wins.
